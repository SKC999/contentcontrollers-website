Privacy Policy for Content Controllers

Last updated: July 3, 2025

Information We Collect:

When you use our app, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address (through Google OAuth authentication)
  • Your YouTube subscription list (to provide app functionality)
  • Basic app usage information (which features you use)
  • User preferences and settings within the app

How We Use Your Information:

  • To authenticate you with Google/YouTube services
  • To customize your YouTube viewing experience
  • To provide the core functionality of hiding recommendations, shorts, comments, and related videos
  • To improve our app's performance and features
  • To maintain user preferences and settings

Data Protection Mechanisms:

We implement the following security measures to protect your sensitive data:

  • Encryption: All data transmission between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Google user data is only accessible by authorized personnel and systems necessary for app functionality
  • OAuth Security: We use Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol, which means we never store or access your Google passwords
  • Data Minimization: We only request and store the minimum data necessary for app functionality
  • Secure Authentication: Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

Data Retention and Deletion:

Google User Data Retention and Deletion Policy:

  • Retention Period: We retain your Google user data only as long as necessary to provide our services, typically for the duration of your active use of the app
  • Automatic Deletion: If you don't use the app for 12 months, your data will be automatically deleted from our systems
  • User-Requested Deletion: You can request immediate deletion of your data by contacting us at chskc999@gmail.com
  • Account Deactivation: When you revoke our app's access through your Google Account settings, we will delete your associated data within 30 days
  • Data Deletion Process: Upon deletion, all your Google user data is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered
  • Backup Data: Any backup copies of your data are also deleted within 90 days of the deletion request

Information Sharing:

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely to provide our app's functionality. We will never share your Google user data with advertisers or marketing companies.

Data Storage:

Your authentication information is handled securely through Google's OAuth systems. We do not store your Google passwords or other sensitive account credentials. All data is stored on secure servers with appropriate encryption and access controls.

Your Rights:

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Revocation: Revoke our app's access to your Google account through your Google Account settings
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

Google API Services:

This app uses Google API Services and is subject to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to This Policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us:

If you have questions about this privacy policy, data deletion requests, or any privacy concerns, please contact us at: chskc999@gmail.com
